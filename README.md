# Data-Science-Project
"Gurgoan House Price Prediction Model"
**Why ARE We Building this?**
Gurgoan, a rapidly growing city in India, has seen a sharp rise in real estate developement over the past decade. With its proximity to Delhi, booming IT hubs, and modern infrastructure,
Gurgoan has become a major attraction for both homebuyers and ivestors. However, the real estate market here is highly dynamic and often difficult to assess without proper data-driven tools.
